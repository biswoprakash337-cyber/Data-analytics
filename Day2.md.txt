PART 1 — WHAT IS DATA CLEANING?

Definition:

PART 5 — EXCEL DATA CLEANING SKILLS
Remove Duplicates

Purpose:
Remove repeated rows.

Location:
Data → Remove Duplicates

TRIM Function

Purpose:
Removes extra spaces.

Formula:
=TRIM(A2)

Importance:
Extra spaces create matching issues.

UPPER / LOWER / PROPER

UPPER:
=UPPER(A2)

Converts text to CAPITAL letters.

LOWER:
=LOWER(A2)

Converts text to small letters.

PROPER:
=PROPER(A2)

Converts text into Proper Case.

Example:
rahul kumar → Rahul Kumar

Find & Replace

Shortcut:
Ctrl + H

Uses:

fix spelling
replace symbols
standardize text
Text to Columns

Purpose:
Separates combined data.

Example:

Rahul,Delhi,5000

Becomes:

Name City Revenue

INTERVIEW QUESTIONS — DAY 2

Q1. What is data cleaning?

Answer:
Data cleaning is the process of identifying and fixing incorrect, incomplete, duplicate, or inconsistent data.

Q2. Why is data cleaning important?

Answer:
Data cleaning ensures accurate analysis and prevents wrong business decisions.

Q3. How do you handle duplicate records?

Answer:
I identify duplicates using tools like Remove Duplicates, filters, or SQL queries and remove them based on business rules.

Q4. What are missing values?

Answer:
Missing values are empty data fields caused by skipped inputs, system issues, or data corruption.

Q5. What problems occur due to inconsistent formatting?

Answer:
Inconsistent formatting causes grouping, filtering, and reporting errors because computers treat values differently.
