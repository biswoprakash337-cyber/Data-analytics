# ***1.What is data Analysis :-***

# ***\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~***

***->Data analysis helps in businesses answer questions using data .***

***->Its main purpose is helps to give right decision to the company at the present time .***



***Example questions like :***

***.Why are sales decreasing?***

***.Which product sells the most?***

***.Which customers are leaving?***

***.Which marketing campaign works best?***

***.Which city gives highest profit?***



***=>Main aim is to converting raw data into business decision .***
